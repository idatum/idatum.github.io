Self hosting Ente Photos with MinIO on FreeBSD

Here are my notes on self hosting [Ente Photos[(https://ente.io) using FreeBSD and MinIO as the S3 compatible backing blob store.

Ente Photos is an app with a backing storage service with a strong focus on privacy. I long ago decided to not use Google Photos on my Android to backup my phone's photos online. Peronsally, I avoid as much as possible Google hoovering up my personal data. I learned about Ente and their support for self hosting its open source server. I decided this was a great way to learn about this service before committing to an Ente storage plan.

Ultimately I'm really happy with the Ente Android photos app and my self hosted server and I'm going to recommend to my family to switch to an Ente subscription to share photos.

Self hosting the Ente server

There is good information on Ente's website for how to host everything on Docker and connect their photos app to your personal server.
